Consumables

Consumables are the heartbeat of the job — the stuff you burn through every single day without thinking about it until you run out. Markers, blades, bits, chalk, tape, batteries, gloves, rags, buckets — all the little things that keep the big things moving. Nobody brags about buying consumables,but every foreman knows the job shuts down fast when this stuff disappears.

These are the items that live in your bags,your gang box,your truck, your pockets, and somehow still go missing by lunch.Cheap, simple,replaceable—but absolutely necessary.This section covers the everyday gear every trade chews through on every job.
