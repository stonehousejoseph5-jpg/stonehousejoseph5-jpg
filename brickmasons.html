"Brick masonry is a trade built on precision,rhythm,and pride".

I learned the craft the old-school way started out under Doug Rigney a man who took care of his crew and made sure we started every morning with steak and eggs. I was taught to lay brick by a left handed mason named Jimmy,which meant he ran one direction and i ran the other until we met in the middle.Fromthere i worked for my uncle Steve at Steve Hooten Masonry, then spent years with Kim Kruse Masonry before joining the Union and starting out as a hod carrier.THese Tools and techniques used by real Masons on real Job-Sites,the same way i learned them: With sweat,skill,and pride in the work
