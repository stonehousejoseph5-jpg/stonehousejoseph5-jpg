Roofers

Roofers handle everything from tear-off to insulation, membrane install, hot tar, flashing, and final seal.Most industrial buildings use rubber (EPDM) or similar membrane systems, sometimes buried under gravel. Roofers need heavy-duty tools built for heat, weight, and nonstop movement across large flat roofs.This list covers the essential gear every roofing crew relies on.
